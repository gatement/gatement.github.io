Vim Plugin: NERDTree

search "NERDTree" in "www.vim.org" to download, unzip and copy to ~/vimfiles(.vim) to install
:helptags ~/vimfiles/doc (let help searchable)

:help nerdtree

:NERDTree [dir|bookmark]

:NERDTreeToggle [dir|bookmark] (C-n)
:NERDTreeFind (C-h)
:NERDTreeMirror (C-l)

:Bookmark  (add bookmark)
:ClearAllBookmarks

--file
o go (open)
i gi (split)
s gs (vsplit)
t T (open in new tabnew)

--dir
o O (open/close, open all sub dir)
x X (close parent, close all sub dir)
e (explore dir)

--bookmark
o t T (open)
D (delete)

--nevigation
p (go to parent dir)
P (go to root)
K (first child)
J (last child)
C-k (prev sibling)
C-j (last sibling)

--fileSystem
C (current as root)
u U (go to upper root (and leave old root open))
r R (refresh dir/root)
m (menu)
cd (change selected node as CWD)

--toggle
I (hidden file)
f (file filter)
F (files)
B (bookmarks)
A (maximize/minimize)
? (help)

--else
q (close tree)
